How Does This Game work?

  1. Get a group of four people and when you are ready press the "Start Game" button.
  2. Use the form to enter your names and start the game!
  3. Once you are in the game you will be shown a card that asks a question, the goal is for each person to vote for the person that they think the card best fits.
  4. The person with the most votes will be crowned the winner of the round and another card will be shown in which the voting will repeat.
  5. After several rounds the person who reaches 2 wins will be crowned the winner of the game and you will be shown the results!
